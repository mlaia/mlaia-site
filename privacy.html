Privacy Policy

This policy explains how MLAIA collects, uses and protects your personal data, in compliance with the Israeli Privacy Protection Law (Amendment 13, effective August 2025) and applicable regulations.

Effective Date August 2025
Last Updated March 2025
Controller MLAIA
Contact yochai@mlaia.com

1. Who We Are

MLAIA (mlaia.com) is an AI and data science consulting firm led by Dr. Yochai Edlitz, based in Israel. As the operator of this website, MLAIA is the data controller responsible for personal data collected through this site.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data you actively provide or that is generated automatically when you use the site:

  • Contact form submissions: name, email address, and the message you write to us.
  • Analytics data: page visits, time on page, referral source, browser type and general geographic region — collected anonymously via Cloudflare analytics. No personal identifiers are linked to analytics data.
  • Cookie preferences: your response to our cookie consent banner (stored locally in your browser).

We do not collect sensitive personal data (health, financial, biometric) through this website.

3. Purpose & Legal Basis

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your contact form inquiries (legal basis: contract performance / legitimate interest).
  • Understanding site usage to improve our services (legal basis: legitimate interest; analytics data is anonymised).
  • Complying with legal obligations under Israeli law.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

  • Contact form messages: retained for up to 3 years from the date of submission, or until the business relationship ends, whichever is later.
  • Analytics data: aggregated, anonymous — retained indefinitely as statistical records.
  • Cookie consent preferences: stored in your browser until you clear your cookies.

5. Your Rights

Under the Israeli Privacy Protection Law and Amendment 13, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

👁
Right of Access
Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
✏️
Right of Correction
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
🗑
Right of Deletion
Request deletion of your personal data where there is no overriding legal obligation to retain it.
📦
Right of Portability
Receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at yochai@mlaia.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

This site uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: required for the site to function (e.g., your language preference, cookie consent choice). These cannot be declined.
  • Analytics cookies: used by Cloudflare to collect anonymous, aggregated traffic data. You can decline these via the cookie consent banner.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings.

7. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss or disclosure. The site is served over HTTPS. Contact form data is transmitted securely.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare: provides DNS, CDN, DDoS protection and anonymous analytics.
  • Google Fonts: fonts are loaded from Google's servers; Google may collect technical data per its privacy policy.

We do not integrate social media trackers, advertising networks, or other third-party tracking services.

9. Contact & Complaints

For any privacy-related question or request, contact:

  • Dr. Yochai Edlitz — yochai@mlaia.com

If you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you may file a complaint with the Israeli Privacy Protection Authority (PPA).
