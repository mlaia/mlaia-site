5. Legal Basis for Processing

Under Israeli law (Privacy Protection Law 5741-1981, as amended by Amendment 13), we process personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent (סעיף הסכמה): for optional analytics cookies and any future marketing communications. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interest (אינטרס לגיטימי): for website analytics (understanding site usage to improve our services), and for retaining contact form messages to facilitate follow-up.
  • Contractual necessity (הכרח חוזי): for processing contact form submissions when you request information about our services, and for delivering consulting services under a client agreement.
  • Legal obligation (חובה חוקית): to comply with obligations under Israeli law, including tax records and regulatory requirements.

6. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data. We share data only with the following service providers, strictly as necessary to operate this website:

  • No other third parties receive your personal data from this website. We do not integrate social media trackers, advertising networks or behavioural analytics platforms.

You may contact us for further details about these safeguards.

9. Your Rights Under Amendment 13

The Israeli Privacy Protection Law (Amendment 13, effective August 2025) grants you the following rights regarding your personal data. These rights apply to data we hold about you as a data subject:

👁
Right of Access
Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, including the purposes of processing and the categories of data involved.
✏️
Right of Correction
Request correction of inaccurate, incomplete or outdated personal data we hold about you.
🗑
Right of Deletion
Request deletion of your personal data where there is no overriding legal obligation or legitimate purpose to retain it.
📦
Right of Portability
Receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format, where technically feasible.
🚫
Right to Withdraw Consent
Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
⏹️
Right to Restrict Processing
Request that we limit how we use your personal data in certain circumstances (e.g., while accuracy is contested or during an objection).
🔰
Right to Object
Object to processing of your personal data where we rely on legitimate interest as the legal basis, including for direct marketing purposes.
⚖️
Right to File a Complaint
If you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you may file a complaint with the Israeli Privacy Protection Authority (PPA — רשות הגנת הפרטיות).

10. How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of the rights listed above, please contact us in writing using the details below. We respond to all verified requests within 30 days as required by the Israeli Privacy Protection Law.

Privacy requests: yochai@mlaia.com — Please include 'Privacy Request' in the subject line and describe the right you wish to exercise. 12. Security Measures

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration or destruction. These measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption: all data transmitted between your browser and this site is encrypted using TLS (Transport Layer Security).
  • Static site hosting: this website is a static site with no server-side database, minimising the attack surface for data breaches.
  • Access controls: access to any personal data received via the contact form is limited to authorised personnel only.
  • Third-party security: we rely on established, industry-standard infrastructure providers (GitHub Pages, Cloudflare) that maintain their own comprehensive security programmes.

No method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that poses a significant risk to your rights, we will notify you and the Israeli Privacy Protection Authority as required by law.
